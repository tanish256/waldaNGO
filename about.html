    • Who We Are

    Walda Children's Foundation Ltd is a committed organization that supports both orphaned and disadvantaged children.
    We believe that no child should be separated from opportunities due to their circumstances. By offering access to education, medical care, food, and clean water, we create a nurturing and hopeful environment for children to thrive.

    Over the years,
    we have seen lives transformed through the generosity of our donors and volunteers. Our foundation is built on the belief that even small contributions can create lasting impact.

    Whether providing food, education, or medical care, our goal is always to make a positive difference in the world, one person at a time.

    We Have Helped Over 200 Children

    Through your generosity, we've been able to provide shelter, food, and education to over 200 Children in need. With your support, we continue to make a lasting impact and transform lives. Together, we can do even more.
